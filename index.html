Seiden Sans is an open-source, sans serif typeface by Kevin Beronilla. Inspired by both neo-grotesque and geometric typefaces, Seiden Sans was created in an attempt to combine the two aesthetics without compromising legibility.

The typeface was created with Adobe Illustrator and BirdFont, and is licensed under the SIL Open Font License.
